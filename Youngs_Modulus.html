Theoretical Reference

Working Formula:
Y = (g · L³ · m) / (4 · b · d³ · l)
Where:
• g = 9.81 m/s² (981 cm/s²)
• L = Knife-edge gap spacing
• b = Breadth of the bar
• d = Depth of the bar
• l = Mean depression produced
